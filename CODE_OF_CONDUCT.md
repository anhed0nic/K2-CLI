# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Khao2 project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy toward others

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Trolling, insulting comments, and personal attacks
- Harassment in any form
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Repeat offenders may be temporarily or permanently banned.

## Reporting

Report violations to odin@odinglynn.com. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
